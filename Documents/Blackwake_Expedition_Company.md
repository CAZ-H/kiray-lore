Blackwake Expedition Company is a research, recovery, and reclamation concern of the highest standing, trusted across settled space to survey the unknown and return it as value. 

We accept the contracts others decline, and we deliver them with a consistency our competitors cannot match. Every engagement is seen through in full, whether it calls for patient science, decisive security, or heavy industry, and whether it is carried out in person or remotely. Our regular clients include heavy commerce and merchant stations, vast frontier outposts, offices of interstellar law, and various private independent partners.

## Our Benefactors

Blackwake's reputation would not be possible without the generosity of our benefactors, whose support makes our diverse capabilities possible.

We are proud to work alongside the finest providers of research, security, and industrial equipment found both on and off of the open interstellar market. Our logistics and maintenance partners keep our fleet reliable and up to any task, far from any port without the discretion we value. Through standing arrangements with a number of correctional and rehabilitative institutions, we are equipped with a steady and willing labor pool for any assignment that requires it. 

Blackwake dearly values the utmost discretion in and around our operations, as do our benefactors. As such, we do not name them here.

## The Fleet

Blackwake fulfills contracts through an extensive network of subcontractor organizations, coordinated from a fleet comprised of **five capital ships**.

Three of these are **Prospector-class** vessels, mobile survey and recovery platforms built to establish themselves in orbit above a target world and work it thoroughly, from first survey to final extraction. The remaining two are **Longhaul-class** vessels, heavier ships dedicated to the long transport of material, personnel, and recovered goods between engagements and onward to the markets that receive them.

Beyond the fleet, Blackwake retains 182 independent subcontractors, unassigned to any ship and available for discreet, single-objective engagements wherever a client requires them. They are the reason no contract is ever truly beyond our reach.

### CS Harrow

The operation above [[Kiray]] is conducted from **CS Harrow**, a Prospector-class vessel and the authoring vessel of this archive.

The Harrow carries a full complement of 312 staff and 62 embedded Blackwake subcontractors: researchers, technicians, commanders, and security personnel who direct every aspect of the Kiray engagement from orbit. She currently maintains a standing complement of 671 sponsored operators as well, participants in Blackwake's Sponsored Service Program, to whom the surface work itself is entrusted.

Kiray's surface is hostile to human life, and its native civilization remains a matter of active recovery, so direct human presence on the ground is not economical. Instead, operators are held in Continuity aboard the Harrow, their cognition projected into purpose-grown vessels and layered over the natural instincts beneath. Our operators live and labor upon the surface as though native to it. Blackwake extends terms of eventual release to its sponsored operators in exchange for this contracted service, entrusting them with assignments where conventional crew cannot reasonably be risked. Field units are consumable by design, and the loss of a unit interrupts, but does not conclude, an operator's term of service.

Operator welfare is a point of pride. Between deployments, operators are afforded rest, nutrition, hygiene, and supervised recreation, comforts Blackwake extends well beyond the requirements of contract. It is through their service that the whole of this archive was gathered.

## The Kiray Archive

This archive is the deliverable of the Kiray engagement: a complete account of the world as it was found, including its geology and climate, its living things, and its once sprawling civilization and what remains of it. This information is recorded here to support any futher operations intended by the partner who commissioned it.

Every entry is prepared to the same exacting standard, so that our commissioning partner may come to know Kiray as intimately as those who walked it in our service, and so that nothing of value is overlooked when the time comes to bring it home.
