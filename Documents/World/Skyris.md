Skyris is a K1V type star that is 80% of the mass of Sol, and about 75% of its radius. The habitable zone of Skyris is within 0.57 - 0.82 AU. It is approximately 5.8 billion years old. 

The temperature of Skyris is about 5155 K. Because it is cooler and smaller than Sol, Skyris burns slowly and steadily, giving it a lifespan far longer than a Sol-type star and a calmer output of harmful radiation. Its light is slightly red-shifted, appearing warm white to pale orange. The luminosity of Skyris is about 36% of Sol.

## The Solar System

The Skyris System consists of 5 major bodies. Only the [[Kiray|moon of the second body]] in the system is capable of sustaining life.

In order from the sun, the Skiedonic people have recorded the bodies in the system under the names: 
1. **Skel:** a hot, volcanic, sulfurous planet.
2. **[[Kete]]:** a striking purple gas giant.
	- Kete hosts [[Kiray]], the subject of this archive.
3. **Siirk:** an arid barren planet, rich in iron, nickel, and silicates.
	- Siirk hosts Si, a small barren moon. 
4. **Vol:** a massive, deep blue gas giant, almost twice the diameter of Kete.
	- Vol hosts the smaller Avo'ton and the larger Avo'rii, two barren moons. The names of these moons simply reflect their numbered positions in [[Skiedonic_Numeral_System|Skiedonic]].
5. **Avo:** a deep striated red gas giant with particulate rings, slightly smaller than Kete. 

Finally, a thin, stable belt of asteroids and space debris encircles the Skyris system.
